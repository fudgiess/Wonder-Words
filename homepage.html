HOW TO PLAY

CATEGORIES:

There are 3 categories in this game, Easy, Moderate, Difficult. Each category contains 10 questions (30 total), wherein you have to answer the given questions. You can do so by pressing the letters one-by-one until you form the correct answer.

POINTS:

Each correct answer gives you 10 points. You can spend points to avail clues, which cost 25 points. You can choose whether you want a vowel clue or a consonant clue. But choose wisely because you only have 3 clues for the entire game.

STRIKES:

You are given three chances; if you make 3 wrong guesses, you lose the entire game! Choose wisely.

Good Luck!
